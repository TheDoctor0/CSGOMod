<font size="4" color="orange"><b>After joining the server, you must log in.<br>
To do this type <font color="#f00">/account</font>.</b></font><br>
<br>
After logging in, all the necessary information will appear in the upper right-hand corner.<br>
<br>
<b><font color ="#ff0000">For every kill and objective of the map, you will get a Euro for which you can get skins!</font></b><br>
<br>
You can use /menu to access all available commands.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>1. Set Skin (/set)</b></font><br>
In this menu you can select your skins and put them on the chosen weapons.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>2. Buy Skin (/kup)</b></font><br>
This is a shop where you can buy skins at a certain price.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>3. Draw Skin (/draw)</b></font><br>
This is the place where the skins can be drawn. Remember, not every draw will give a skin.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>4. Skins Market (/market)</b></font><br>
In this menu you can put your skins up for sale or buy some from other players.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>5. Operations (/operations)</b></font><br>
Here you can perform various operations - missions for which you will receive additional funds.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>6. Clans (/clans)</b></font><br>
Clans are your very own squads for players, which give you special bonuses.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>7. Transfer Money (/transfer)</b></font><br>
The option to transfer the Euro to other players.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>8. Exchange Skin (/exchange)</b></font><br>
Anybody have a skin you want? It's in this menu you can trade with him.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>9. Give Skin (/give)</b></font><br>
If you've got a good heart, you can give someone the skin you don't need.<br>
<br>
<font size="4" color="orange"><b>10. HUD Settings (/hud)</b></font><br>
You can change the HUD display settings here.<br>
